About Pixelated Me

"Pixelated Me: Code Labyrinth" is a 2D 2D pixel maze platformer, blending problem-solving and retro gaming with a unique twist. You, as a developer, navigate a pixelated character through a coding-themed maze. This game brings the worlds of coding and retro gaming together,creating a unique and immersive experience. This game was created as part of my second-year coursework for Middlesex University's CST2120 Web Application and Databases module in 2023. I hope it brings you as much joy to play as it brought me tears to create :').
